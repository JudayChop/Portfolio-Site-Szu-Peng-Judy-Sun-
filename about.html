About

My Story

Hi there! My name is Szu Peng Sun, but I go by Judy. I’m a UX/UI Designer with a strong hospitality and retail background. I studied Hotel & Lodging Management in college and worked at various hotels, restaurants, cafes, and now in retail. I stumbled upon UX/UI design when I was researching for a new career that challenges me more creatively. I’ve always been into music and the arts since I was young and been told I have a good artistic eye. I actually went to a performing arts high school in New York City (the fame school), I was a Vocal major. Which means, I love to sing! Besides singing, I enjoy hiking, traveling, photography, and cuddling with my cat.

I’ve worked on various UX/UI projects at CareerFoundry, and also currently learning Frontend Development for Designers (HTML, CSS, Javascript). Because of my strong hospitality and retail background, I easily empathize with people. I can also identify customer needs and quickly resolve them efficiently. This ties in to creating user-centric designs for UX/UI design. My goal is to make a positive impact on people’s lives while creating beautiful designs.
